TWICE

Lets go to know real artists?
This is TWICE, a korean girlgroup of kpop song. They started their career in October 20, 2015, and theyre still active in the in the company JYP Entertainment.

Twice is formed by 9 women:

  • Jihyo, the leader of the group.
  • Nayeon, the visual, eldest and main vocalist.
  • Jeongyeon, also visual but sub vocalist.
  • Momo, main dancer, sub vocalist and sub rapper.
  • Sana, sub vocalist and also visual (the prittiest, in my opnion;)).
  • Mina, also main dancer and sub vocalist.
  • Dahyun, main rapper, sub vocalist and also visual.
  • Chaeyoung, also main rapper and sub vocalist.
  • Tzuyu, main visual, sub vocalist and "maknae" (youngest, in korean).
